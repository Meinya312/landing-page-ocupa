ABOUT PROGRAMS HOACDD RULES & POLICIES
The Office of the City Urban Poor Affairs (OCUPA) was established to assist and support urban poor communities by providing essential services, managing beneficiary records, and facilitating access to various programs.
The office plays a crucial role in ensuring that underprivileged families receive housing assistance, financial aid, and other social services.
Empowering urban poor communities through innovative programs, transparent governance, and accessible services. OCUPA bridges the gap between government support and community needs.
As a partner of the Department of Human Settlements and Urban Development (DHSUD) at the Lingkod HOA Desk, we provided assistance to eighteen (18) homeowners associations or walk-in clients.
