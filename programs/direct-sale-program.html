Direct Sale Program for the Informal Settler Families (ISF) and Urban Poor Association

The City Government of Lucena formally acquired the parcels of land from landowner. Residents in the areas will be able to own land where they have lived for decades. The acquired lots will be awarded to the residents through a Direct Sale Program under the scheme, the City Government of Lucena will buy the property from the landowner then the beneficiaries will then secure their individual title after payment of their respective lots to the city government. The fund will be depending on the availability of local funds.
Office or Division: Organization Services Division/Office of the City Urban Poor Affairs
Classification: Highly Technical
Type of Transaction: G2C - Government to Citizen
G2B - Government to Business Entity/ies
G2G - Government to Government
Who may avail: Informal Settler Families (on-sites) and landowner of private property
