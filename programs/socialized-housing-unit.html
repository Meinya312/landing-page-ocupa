Availment of Socialized Housing Program: Application of Socialized Housing Unit under Resettlement Assistance Program for Local Government Unit (RAP-LGUs) of National Housing Authority (NHA)

The City Government of Lucena, through its Office of the City Urban Poor Affairs, implements the Resettlement Assistance Program (RAP) for Local Government Units (LGUs) under the National Housing Authority (NHA), which provides socialized housing units to qualified Informal Settler Families (ISF).
Office or Division: Housing Services Division/Office of the City Urban Poor Affairs
Classification: Highly Technical
Type of Transaction: G2C - Government to Citizen
G2G - Government to Government
Who may avail: Informal Settler Families (ISF)
Urban Poor Association
